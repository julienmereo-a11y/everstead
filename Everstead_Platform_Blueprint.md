# Everstead Platform Blueprint

## 1. Executive Summary
Everstead is a trust-first platform that helps people prepare the practical side of death or incapacity by organizing accounts, documents, instructions, wishes, and family handoff plans in one secure place.

Core promise: make a hard moment easier, clearer, and less chaotic.

Core emotional position: this is not about fear; it is about reducing stress for the people you love.

## 2. Positioning

### What Everstead Does
- Organizes digital life and legal essentials
- Stores critical records and planning details
- Enables controlled sharing with trusted people
- Guides families, executors, and advisors through next steps

### Primary Audiences
- Adults planning ahead
- Parents
- Aging adults
- Families supporting elderly relatives
- Executors
- Estate planners, lawyers, and financial advisors

### Sharp MVP Narrative
A secure family handoff platform for accounts, documents, and instructions.

## 10. Brand and Design Direction

### Visual Language
- Calm, premium, trust-first
- Soft neutral backgrounds
- Deep navy/charcoal text
- Muted accents (blue, sage, slate)
- Generous spacing
- Rounded cards
- Minimal icon style
- Light motion only where helpful

### Voice and Tone
- Reassuring
- Private
- Practical
- Respectful
- Non-alarmist

Avoid aggressive urgency, overly cheerful startup language, morbid imagery, and technical jargon overload.
